Introduction
What is Matthews Overton?
Matthews Overton is the Birmingham office of Matthews Development — a boutique, locally-rooted economic development practice working across the city and the broader Magic City region. We partner with City Hall, county leadership, foundations, anchor institutions, and neighborhood operators to design strategies that move capital into the corridors and communities most often overlooked by it. We are deliberately small, deliberately local, and deliberately patient — and deliberately backed.

A few commitments we hold to.

  1. Patient capital.

    We measure projects in decades, not exits. The right project, well structured, should still be working a generation from now.

  2. Local first.

    We start with the people already doing the work in a place. Outside capital should accelerate local conviction — not replace it.

  3. Honest math.

    Pro formas, public benefits, and political costs all on the same page. If the numbers only work with optimism, the project doesn’t.

  4. Aligned incentives.

    Public, private, and philanthropic capital each want different things. Our job is to design deals where everyone can keep their word.

  5. Quiet ambition.

    The best community work is rarely the loudest. We’re happy for the place to be the headline.
